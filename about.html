We provide future of energy

We are Nyika Solar Technologies; we focus on manufacturing and selling of solar water heaters, Solar lighting and Solar Power supply.


It is NSTâ€™s intention to see that there is universal access to sustainable, reliable, and renewable energy in Malawi and Southern/Eastern Africa.


The business currently exists as a consolidated plan comprising of technocrats in Engineering and Solar technologies with cumulative years of experience of over 50 years working in developed countries such as United Kingdom, United States of America, Canada, and developing countries such as South Africa. The consortium has the support of Solar Dynamics Limited from Barbados as a strategic partner to offer proprietary technologies in the manufacturing of solar water heater products in Malawi. The company will primarily manufacture solar water heaters with a 60 degrees Centigrade temperature guarantee on a 24/7 basis as a unique feature. Other products to be produced include solar lights and solar power supplies. The target market includes households and corporate institutions with a bigger market share within the tourism and education sector offering solutions to lodges, hotels, hospitals/clinics, boarding schools and Universities. The project's factory site has been duly identified in Lilongwe, Malawi, and currently awaiting financial resources to commence factory set up. The company is duly registered with the Registrar of companies in Malawi and it possesses a duly executed Memorandum of Understanding and a 10-Year Technology Transfer Agreement with Solar Dynamics Limited
